16 "Wait -hold -wait, runner. Don't go cold,
Summer. Day, week, our era;
Quick hold it!

17 Wake -hold -wait, Summer, grow corn, wide,
thicker wait hold, fire flicker...

Quick hold it!"

18 The taste wanes dull the scent runs weak.
I kept your coin but the quoin won't keep,
19 and all I know is leaving me
-that fiery sword's cleft everything!

20 "But somewhere between 12 and 1 out from the
clock a new theme sprung!

21 ...For now our time moves in a line;
2 before and 1 behind..."
